Nam Prik Ong

Home

Description

Nam phrik ong is a popular Thai food in Northern Thailand. It is often paired with sticky rice, pork and fresh vegetables such as cucumber, lentils, Chinese cabbage etc.

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillet
  • Equal parts tomato
  • 5-8 tbsp Chili Paste
  • Canola Oil
  • 2-3 Tbsp Garlic
  • 1/2 Cup Water
  • Pinch of Sugar
  • Chicken Boullion
  • Lime Juice

Procedure

  1. Mince Salmon fillet
  2. Mince/ Chop tomato
  3. Mix simon and tomato in bowl
  4. Add chili paste
  5. Heat oil in pan
  6. Add garlic to hot pan
  7. When slightly brown, add salmon/tomato mixture
  8. With residual salmon in bowl, add water
  9. When mostly cooked, add water/salmon mixture
  10. Add sugar and chicken boullion
  11. Finish with lime
