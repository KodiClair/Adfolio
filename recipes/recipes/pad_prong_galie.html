Pad Prong Galie

Description

The Thai dish known as Bu Pad Pong Karee is a rich and flavorful stir fry of crab with curry powder, egg, milk, green onions, and Chinese celery.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Oyster Sauce (1 tsp for 1 egg)
  • Soy Sauce (1 tsp for 1 egg)
  • Evaporated Milk (50 ml for 1 egg)
  • 1 tsp Sugar
  • Shrimp
  • 1 tbsp Chicken Boullion
  • 1 tbsp Burnt chili paste
  • 4-5 Cloves Garlic
  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • Curry Powder
  • Whole Onion
  • Red Chilis
  • Asian Celery

Procedure

  1. Mix eggs with soy sauce, oyster sauce, evaporated milk, sugar, chicken boullion in a bowl
  2. In a pan, add oil with garlic. After garlic cooks for a bit, add curry powder
  3. Immediately add onions and shrimp
  4. Fully cook shrimp before lowering heat
  5. Add chili paste and oil to pan and incorporate
  6. Lower heat and add eggs to pan, stirring occasionally
  7. Turn off hea, add celery and chilis
  8. Serve hot
